03. The Ethical Dilemma

Identity vs. Data

Should a singer's vocal timbre be protected like a fingerprint? Current copyright laws are struggling to keep up with RVC (Retrieval-based Voice Conversion) technologies.

Economic Shift

By 2028, it is estimated that 20% of library music for TV/Film will be AI-generated, potentially displacing thousands of entry-level composers.
